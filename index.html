Meet our product!

iCheck is a mobile application designed to guide patients through all medical issues, beginning from the symptoms and ending with the right solution. There are two ways of logging in: as a patient and as a doctor. Patients can click part of the human body model that bothers them, choose their symptoms and get the most probable causes, recommendations and precise doctors who might help, book an appointment with them (online/offline) and in case of online consultation get a prescription/recommendations from doctors inside the app. Or it is possible just to ask questions in the forum to get answers from several doctors or other patients (managed by the question author).
